Projects

Duo Security

As part of Duo Hack Day 18, worked on a team of eight engineers to add support for Face ID by leveraging the WebAuthn API to register and authenticate credentials. Eliminates dependence on Duo Push for millions of devices running iOS 14. Coming soon to the Duo Prompt!

Birth Detroit

Ahead of the opening of Birth Detroit Care, constructed a sustainable business model that captures long-term utilization, revenue, and operating expenses. Additionally, analyzed engagement rates and advertising ROI in order to deliver a cohesive social media strategy.

Detroit Tigers

Developed three separate forecasting models (before-season, same-day, and monthly) to predict attendance of Tigers home games. Gathered eight years of data by web scraping with Python, then selected variables based on consistency, MAPE, and reducing multicollinearity.
